 # marketing-plan.md

## Positioning
1-line: Idea-Forge - AI-Powered Ideation Tool for Indie Hackers and Creators

Alternatives:
1. Idea-Forge - The Ideation Partner for Independent Innovators
2. Idea-Forge - Your AI-Driven Idea Generator for Next-Level Creativity
3. Idea-Forge - Unleash Your Potential with AI-Assisted Ideation

## ICP (Ideal Customer Profile)
### Persona 1 - Indie Hacker: John Doe, Software Developer, Budget: $100/month
John is a self-taught software developer who has been building and launching indie projects for the past 5 years. He's always looking for new ideas to work on but often struggles with the ideation process. John is tech-savvy, active on online communities, and has a budget for tools that can help him streamline his workflow.

### Persona 2 - Creative Entrepreneur: Jane Smith, Graphic Designer, Budget: $50/month
Jane is a freelance graphic designer who is always on the lookout for unique and innovative ideas to incorporate into her projects. She finds the ideation process challenging and often relies on brainstorming sessions with colleagues. Jane is budget-conscious but values tools that can help her stand out in a competitive market.

### Persona 3 - Side Hustler: Alex Johnson, Web Developer, Budget: $20/month
Alex is a full-time employee who works on his web development side hustle during his free time. He's always looking for new ideas to work on but finds it hard to dedicate time to the ideation process. Alex is price-sensitive but willing to invest in tools that can help him generate ideas efficiently.

## Channels
1. Online Communities (Reddit, Indie Hackers, Hacker News) - CAC: $5/customer
2. Content Marketing (Blog, YouTube, Podcast) - CAC: $10/customer
3. Social Media Ads (LinkedIn, Twitter, Facebook) - CAC: $20/customer

## Content Cadence
Week 1: Launch Blog Post - Introduction to Idea-Forge
Week 2: YouTube Tutorial - How to Use Idea-Forge for Ideation
Week 3: Podcast Interview - Discussing the Challenges of Ideation and How Idea-Forge Helps
Week 4: Case Study - Success Stories from Indie Hackers and Creators Using Idea-Forge
Week 5: Webinar - Live Demo and Q&A Session
Week 6: Email Campaign - Promote the Webinar and Share Success Stories
Week 7: Blog Post - Tips and Tricks for Getting the Most Out of Idea-Forge
Week 8: YouTube Tutorial - Advanced Features of Idea-Forge

## Launch Milestones
- D-30: Finalize product features, complete onboarding process, and prepare marketing materials
- D-0: Launch Idea-Forge, send welcome emails to early adopters, and start content marketing campaign
- D+30: Analyze user feedback, make necessary adjustments, and continue content marketing
- D+90: Evaluate success metrics (DAU, $MRR target), adjust marketing strategy if necessary, and plan for future product improvements

## Success Metrics
- DAU (Daily Active Users): 500 by D+30, 1000 by D+90
- $MRR (Monthly Recurring Revenue) Target by D+90: $10,000
