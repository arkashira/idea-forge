# Customer Journey Map – Idea‑Forge

| Phase   | Trigger Event | Friction Points | User Emotions | Opportunities to Delight | Key Metric |
|---------|----------------|-----------------|---------------|--------------------------|------------|
| **Aware** | • Indie hacker sees a tweet from a thought‑leader about “AI‑driven idea validation” <br>• Blog post on “Top 10 tools for indie creators” lists Idea‑Forge | • Information overload: dozens of ideation tools on the web <br>• Unclear value proposition (what’s unique about Idea‑Forge?) | Curious, overwhelmed | • **Micro‑content**: 15‑sec explainer video on the landing page that instantly shows a “one‑click idea generation” demo.<br>• **Social proof**: Showcase 3‑digit user‑count badge and a carousel of creator testimonials. | **Click‑through rate (CTR)** from landing page to demo page |
| **Consider** | • User signs up for a free trial or downloads a white‑paper “How to Validate Your First SaaS Idea” | • Sign‑up friction: too many fields, no clear next step <br>• Fear of data privacy (sharing personal idea details) | Skeptical, hopeful | • **Progressive onboarding**: 3‑step wizard that asks only essential data (industry, pain point). <br>• **Privacy guarantee**: “All ideas are stored locally and never shared.” <br>• **Instant value**: Show a sample idea + validation score within 30 s. | **Free‑trial activation rate** (users who complete the wizard) |
| **Try** | • User receives the first AI‑generated idea set and validation score | • AI output feels generic or irrelevant <br>• Limited ability to tweak prompts or refine ideas | Frustrated, curious | • **Prompt‑engineering playground**: Allow users to tweak the prompt in real time and see updated ideas instantly. <br>• **Idea‑validation widgets**: Quick “market fit” score, TAM estimate, and competitor snapshot. <br>• **Community hub**: Share ideas in a public gallery for feedback. | **Time to first actionable idea** (minutes from login to first idea) |
| **Adopt** | • User decides to build a MVP around a validated idea | • Integration pain: exporting ideas to Trello/Notion <br>• Lack of roadmap guidance | Confident, excited | • **Export & sync**: One‑click export to popular project‑management tools. <br>• **Roadmap generator**: Turn the idea into a 12‑week sprint plan with milestones. <br>• **Mentor chat**: Live chat with a community mentor for quick feedback. | **Monthly active users (MAU)** who have exported at least one idea |
| **Expand** | • User launches MVP, sees early traction, wants to iterate | • Scaling issues: AI model latency, cost of premium features <br>• Need for deeper analytics | Empowered, ambitious | • **Advanced analytics**: Cohort analysis, churn prediction, revenue forecasting. <br>• **Premium AI tier**: Faster inference, higher‑quality prompts, priority support. <br>• **Referral program**: Earn credits for inviting other creators. | **Revenue per user (ARPU)** and **Referral conversion rate** |
